Welcome to our Online Food Ordering System! This platform is the result of a collaborative project undertaken in the Database Laboratory at Isfahan University of Technology.


Our team, consisting of Shirin Behnaminia, Parto Sabzevari, and Narges Montazeri, has developed this system to streamline and enhance the food ordering process. The primary goal of this project is to apply our theoretical knowledge of database management in a practical setting, creating a functional and user-friendly application that meets modern requirements.


Through this project, we aimed to integrate various database techniques, ensuring efficient data handling and seamless user experiences. We hope our system demonstrates the potential of database applications in real-world scenarios, particularly in the food and beverage industry.


Thank you for visiting, and we hope you enjoy exploring our project!
